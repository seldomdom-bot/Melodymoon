Music with Nature

A voice shaped
by the land

Melody Moon is a Surf Coast Victorian songwriter, recording artist, producer and performer. Her love of nature and wellbeing have shaped her creative evolution — from folk to mantra, to entwining local nature samples into pop beats, exploring new territory with each release as she champions a re-connection with our own true nature along the way.

Her debut EP 'Little Hopes' (2010) saw 'Out of the City' hit the top 10 on the Amrap AirIt Charts, becoming one of the most played tracks on community radio in Australia. She set off in her kombi touring the East Coast — a welcomed guest on radio shows, at festivals, cosy venues and high-vibed events.

"Just wonderful." — Sophie Longden, ABC Radio NSW

Her songs have been used in campaigns for Environment Victoria and in the Wadawurrung women's surfing film Delama Warri. Nearly 15 years and 6 music releases later, Melody's quest to grow her impact through music continues — her wish is to use all the superpowers she has gathered to empower girls and women's voices.
