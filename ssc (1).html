The Secret Songwriters Club

A safe, magical space
to find your voice

Founded by Melody Moon in 2024, the SSC empowers girls aged 8–12 through voice. It's a mysterious collective that meets weekly on the Surf Coast — a nurturing space where sensitive, creative girls tune in to their inner voice and are truly heard.

Supported by connection with other sensitive creatives, they alchemise life's experiences into magical songs. Their music contains potent messages and reminders from their hearts — tools to help them navigate life into their teenage years and beyond.

This unique project gives young female voices a positive, early experience in the music industry: from co-writing songs through to performance, recording, making video clips and releasing their music. They build the confidence to share their voice in the world and be heard.

Their songs also deeply move those who hear them — to reconnect with their own hearts and true nature.

What they do

The full journey

🎵

Express & Connect

Girls check in with how they're truly feeling and support each other. No feeling is too big or too small — everything becomes creative fuel.

✍️

Write Together

Led by Melody, the group co-writes original songs rooted in their real experiences — turning anxiety, joy, and wonder into music.

🎤

Perform & Record

Songs are performed at community events and recorded professionally at Melody's home studio in Deans Marsh, Victoria.

🎬

Create Video Clips

The girls bring their songs to life visually — making music videos that showcase their creativity and unique point of view.

🌿

Connect with Nature

Sessions weave in time outdoors — drawing inspiration from the natural world of the Surf Coast as a grounding creative practice.

💿

Release Music

Their work is released into the world — on streaming platforms and as a full album — giving girls a real stake in the music industry.
