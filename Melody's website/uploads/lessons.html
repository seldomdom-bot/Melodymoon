Singing & Songwriting Lessons

A lesson made
just for you

Melody is passionate about giving girls and women the held space, guidance, tools and growing courage for their voice to expand — allowing their wisdom and unique essence to be experienced and expressed in the world.

One-on-one lessons are holistic and playful. Expand your range, voice quality, breath, expression and power to sing and create how you want to. Techniques are woven in naturally — because the best learning happens when you're having fun.

Guided by Melody's 30 years of experience in singing, songwriting, recording and performing — along with her highly intuitive nature — she creates a unique path for you with enjoyable, empowering steps towards your goal.

Allowing your wisdom and unique essence to be experienced and expressed in the world.

What you'll explore

Holistic lessons for
every voice

🎵

Voice & Range

Expand your vocal range, tone quality and power — finding the full expression of your unique instrument in a safe, encouraging space.

🌬

Breath & Technique

Learn how breath supports your voice — building stamina, control and ease so singing feels natural and free, not forced.

✍️

Songwriting

Turn your feelings, experiences and stories into songs. Whether you're a beginner or have been writing for years, Melody meets you where you are.

🎸

Instrument Skills

Ukulele and keyboard can be woven in to support your songwriting and performance — no prior experience needed.

🌟

Confidence & Expression

Performance nerves, stage presence and the courage to be heard — each lesson builds the inner confidence that carries beyond music.

🌿

Your Unique Path

No two lessons look the same. Melody's intuitive approach means every session is tailored to where you are and where you want to go.
